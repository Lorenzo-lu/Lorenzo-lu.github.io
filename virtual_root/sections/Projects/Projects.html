The damping factor, coming from the particle size, will reduce the scattering intensity at large angles. It is obtained by calculating sum of the radial mass distribution around each mass unit. To invesitigate it, each arbitrary mass units pair in that particle will be involved. In 3D case, the time complexity is O(N^6). In this project, I introduced the Mento Carlo Method to approximate the damping factor with an O(N^3) complexity.
